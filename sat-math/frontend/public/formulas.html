DSAT Math — Formula Sheet

Common formulas you’re allowed to know and use. Not exhaustive.

Algebra

  • Point–slope form: \( y - y_1 = m(x - x_1) \)
  • Slope from two points: \( m = \frac{y_2 - y_1}{x_2 - x_1} \)
  • Slope–intercept: \( y = mx + b \)
  • Quadratic formula: \( x = \frac{-b \pm \sqrt{b^2 - 4ac}}{2a} \)
  • Vertex of \( ax^2+bx+c \): \( x_v = -\tfrac{b}{2a} \)
  • Exponent rules: \( a^m a^n = a^{m+n} \), \( (a^m)^n = a^{mn} \), \( a^{-n}=\tfrac{1}{a^n} \)
  • Log rules: \( \log_b(xy)=\log_b x+\log_b y \), \( \log_b(x^k)=k\log_b x \)

Advanced Math

  • System 2×2 (determinant idea): if \( ad-bc \neq 0 \), unique solution
  • Completing the square: \( x^2+bx=(x+\tfrac{b}{2})^2-\tfrac{b^2}{4} \)
  • Exponential form: \( y=a\,b^x \) and \( \log_b y = x \iff b^x=y \)
  • Rational equation tip: multiply both sides by LCD; check extraneous roots

Geometry & Trig

  • Pythagorean theorem: \( a^2+b^2=c^2 \)
  • Triangle interior sum: \( A+B+C=180^\circ \)
  • Right triangle defs: \( \sin\theta=\tfrac{\text{opp}}{\text{hyp}} \), \( \cos\theta=\tfrac{\text{adj}}{\text{hyp}} \), \( \tan\theta=\tfrac{\text{opp}}{\text{adj}} \)
  • Rect. perimeter/area: \( P=2(w+h) \), \( A=wh \)
  • Circle: \( C=2\pi r \), \( A=\pi r^2 \)
  • Distance: \( d=\sqrt{(x_2-x_1)^2+(y_2-y_1)^2} \)
  • Midpoint: \( M=\big(\tfrac{x_1+x_2}{2},\tfrac{y_1+y_2}{2}\big) \)

Problem Solving & Data Analysis

  • Percent change: \( \tfrac{\text{new}-\text{old}}{\text{old}}\times 100\% \)
  • Mean: \( \bar{x}=\tfrac{1}{n}\sum x_i \); Weighted mean: \( \bar{x}=\tfrac{\sum w_i x_i}{\sum w_i} \)
  • Unit rate: \( \tfrac{\text{quantity}}{\text{time or units}} \)
  • Line of best fit: slope interprets rate of change per unit in \(x\)
Tip: Always check units and reasonableness of answers.
